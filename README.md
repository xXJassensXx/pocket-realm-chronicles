## Project Status

This story is currently in **early development**, with most work focused on **planning, outlining, and concept exploration**. The project is still taking shape, and many worldbuilding elements, characters, and plot structures are being refined.

The central concept driving the creation of this story is the **Pocket Realm**, a unique space that serves as the narrative's core idea and the foundation for many of its themes, abilities, and conflicts.

As development continues, this repository will be updated with drafts, outlines, worldbuilding notes, and other materials that illustrate the progression from concept to full narrative.

## Recommended Reading Setup

This project is being created and organized in **Obsidian**, which allows for a flexible, non-linear approach to storytelling and worldbuilding.  
For the **best reading experience**, it is recommended to **import the repository into Obsidian** and explore the story and related notes from there.  
This way, you can follow arcs, characters, and worldbuilding entries in a way that reflects the structure and connections intended by the author.
Link to the Obsidian Website: https://obsidian.md/
